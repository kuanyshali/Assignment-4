About us


The Astana IT University is home to a talented group of undergraduate students, each with their unique passions and skills. Let's introduce us briefly:

Kuanyshali Aldabergen is a 19-year-old chess master hailing from Almaty. His strategic mind and dedication to the game have made him a force to be reckoned with.

Merei Zhumabek, born and raised in Astana, is an avid sports enthusiast. While he used to play football, hockey, and basketball, he now finds joy in hitting the gym and exploring the world of cars. In his free time, he enjoys watching movies, TV series, and cruising the streets with music.

Adlet Zhenisov possesses a strong foundation in programming languages such as Java and C++. His excellent communication skills, adaptability, and ability to work effectively in teams make him a valuable asset to any project.

Amirzhan Daulet, originally from Taldykorgan, found his passion in web development. Together with a friend, he has created engaging domestic website pages. His journey led him to Astana, where he obtained his passport and ID documents.

We are group of Astana IT University students showcase a diverse range of talents and aspirations, coming together to contribute to the vibrant academic environment.
